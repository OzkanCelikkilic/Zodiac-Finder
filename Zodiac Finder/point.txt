<Aquarius>
Aquarius is the eleventh astrological sign in the Zodiac. People born between January 20 and February 18 are considered to be under this sign. Aquarius is symbolized by the Water Bearer, and its ruling planet is Uranus. Individuals with the Aquarius sign are often known for their independent and progressive nature. They are often seen as intellectual, friendly, and humanitarian.
</Aquarius>
